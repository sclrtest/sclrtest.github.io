Legal Activities

Our Legal Activities

We are a law firm specialized in commercial and custom law. Our law office provides legal advice to international and domestic companies, in their commercial or industrial activities in Kosovo or abroad through proactive legal advice as well as by the representation of our clients in courts.

We have been involved in a number of the significant business transactions, business representations and privatizations in Kosovo. We find solutions to complex issues of law and jurisdiction often involving the interplay between government agencies and commercial organizations, national and international.

The firm's attorneys have extensive knowledge of the Kosovo legislation as well as understanding of business in Kosovo.

Our law firm provides superior, cost-efficient legal services to a wide range of domestic and international clients in the following areas: Corporate Law, Custom Law, Mergers and Acquisitions, Property Law, Tax, Labor and Employment, Trademarks and Litigation.

Our law firm has the expertise and is experienced in providing the whole range of legal, financial and market research services in both initial investment phase (Privatization, Establishment of Companies and Representative Offices) and the post-investment phase, helping the investors to achieve and maintain development and growth of their businesses in Kosovo.

Working individually or in teams, we remain committed in providing best support and assistance to our clients in English and Serbian language.
