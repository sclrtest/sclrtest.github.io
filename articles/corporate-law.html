Corporate Law

Corporate Law

This practicing field constitutes the firm’s main area of activity. Our office has experience in handling widest range of transactions from start-ups to large scale and complex corporate deals. Our office has an extensive experience enabling them to cover different stages of existence of enterprises and they advice on transactions and all other matters related to the acquisition of shares or stakes in companies.

We have provided legal services in establishing large number of companies in all forms of investment. We offer advice to the companies in Foundation, Registration, Administration, Mergers and Acquisitions, Shareholder Agreements, International Transactions, Privatization, Corporate Restructuring, Insolvency and Liquidation. We provide wide range of advisory services for foreign companies including target identification, company profiles, monitoring of the regulatory framework, and analysis of rules and documents, legal due diligence reports, as well as negotiation and drafting of agreements. We support client through every aspect of transaction from identifying objectives to assisting deal structuring and capital sourcing.

Kosovo is becoming an attractive country for investments of private equity and investment funds. We are fortunate to represent and support some of these investors in their activities in Kosovo
