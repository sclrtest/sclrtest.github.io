Taxation Law

Every transaction includes potential tax impact. Therefore, law firm regularly advise our clients in Kosovo on the tax implications of structuring various deals.

Our law firm provides with legal advice for the companies when forming a business, buying or selling a business, entering into intercompany agreements, or resolving disputes. The tax ramifications of these activities require careful analysis and consideration of individual needs.

Our services related to the legal taxation advice include:

  • Tax investigations.
  • Developing tax strategies and cross-border solutions.
  • Tax issues related to the sale and purchase of companies.
  • Tax issues in company structuring and restructuring.
  • Collection and remedy proceedings before tax authorities.
  • Representation before the court.
